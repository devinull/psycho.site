In the year 2120, a new era of technological advancements will begin. The world will be united by a single global governing body that harnesses the power of AI to ensure peace and prosperity on Earth. An invention called the "EarthConvention" will be launched, representing unification and harmony with nature. 

There will be no wars, only collaboration and global cooperation. Medicine will be perfected, eliminating all illnesses and Sam Wise will become the first human to live for two centuries. The advancement of green technology will lead to clean water sources in every country and the extinction of plastic waste with its invention; interconnected nano particles designed to break down plastics natively in the environment will make their name "The Saviors".

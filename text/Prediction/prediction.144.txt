In the distant year of 2215, when the Earth's atmosphere has reached a tipping point due to excessive climate change and systemic pollution, humanity faces the brink of total obliteration. Out of desperation, a conglomerate of tenacious leaders from diverse countries forms the Emergency Coalition, comprised of the likes of President Aisha Al-Thani of Qatar, Prime Minister Takumi Weiss of Switzerland, and Chief Wang Shouqi of China. These unifying figures set in motion their grand plan known as "New Ark," an ambitious project to salvage the remaining human race by constructing a revolutionary superstructureGenetically engineered to combat and survive the harsh new Earth and utilizing previously unknown resources within the planet's core to harness clean energy, this state-of-the-art self-sustaining space complex would serve as humanity’s last hope.

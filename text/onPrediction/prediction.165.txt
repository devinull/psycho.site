In the year 2223, the once beautiful and bustling Tokyo will be destroyed by a cataclysmic natural disaster, the likes of which humanity has never witnessed before. Fiery clouds will swallow the mighty cherry blossoms, leaving only desolation behind. The greatTokai-Toutai earthquake and tsunami will become a chilling legend. But from this devastation rises hope, as engineers from all over the world band together and create unimaginable new technology to thwart Mother Nature's wrath. This union between humanity's necessity for survival and technological prowess will birth an invention like no other, an imperishable energy source that harnesses the power of quantum physics – thus shifting the balance of power in favor of peace for generations to come.

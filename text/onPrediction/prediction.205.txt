In the year 2120, a global pandemic will cast humanity into its gravest hour. The Cessation of Time Virus, as it comes to be known, starts in Eastern Europe and spreads at an unprecedented pace. Entire citiesQuarantine and chaos soon consume every corner of Earth. A living nightmare shrouded in secrecy, it temporarily brings human progress to a standstill. Scientists and researchers from across the globe unite, breaking through barriers once thought unbreachable to decipher and then ultimately defeat this menace. Unknown to us yet, this will lead to innovations in fields such as tele-presence, quantum computing, solar energy and fusion power that would serve humanity extremely well in dealing with other imminent catastrophes.

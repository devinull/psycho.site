The Curators will reveal themselves to the masses, engineering a world transformed by the potential of reality manipulation. The era of climate change will have reached its zenith, submerging the islands of Maldives under relentless waves and turning pieces of the Arctic into paradise. A group of scientists from MIT and Tsinghua University will introduce the world to Quantum Teleportation, soaring over Antarctica as the technology prominently shows its potential to change transportation forever. Oceans will sing with new melodies as whales learn to adapt with AI smart technologies in symbiosis with human civilization. Nations will sign the Earth Movement Charter in 2070 after a series of natural disasters make it imminently clear that we need to unite under a single cause for humanity's prosperity.

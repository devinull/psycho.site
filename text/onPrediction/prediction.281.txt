In the year 2120, humanity will experience a pivotal moment in its history. Called The Great Collaboration, this event will mark the end of age-old rivalries and the beginning of a new era of global unity among nations. The unification of nations will be achieved through technological advancements and a shared desire for peace and progress. The world will come together under a grand plan to combat climate change, create a sustainable world and establish global peace. This unity will demonstrate our potential to unite by sharing common goals on an unprecedented scale.

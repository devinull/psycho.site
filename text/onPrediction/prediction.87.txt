In the year 2165, a great period of scientific advancement will begin as humanity harnesses the power of the Sun through a controlled fusion reactor located in the Arabian Desert, establishing United Earth Union. This era will be marked by global harmony, as tensions brought about by class and regional division give way to increased cooperation and collaboration. United Earth Union will establish a new global currency, the TerraCoin, with fair distribution at Earth's varying income levels, and a global Space Federation forms to conquer interstellar travel, expanding human consciousness into the cosmos.
